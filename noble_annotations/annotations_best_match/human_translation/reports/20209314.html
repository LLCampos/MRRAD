ABSTRACT The objective of this Brazilian consensus is to update and to continue the standardization of the principal terms and fundamental patterns in scans in Portuguese. There is a succinct definition of the principal terms used to describe findings, as well as illustrations of classic examples. The group of authors comprised radiologists specializing in radiology and membership in the Brazilian College of Radiology and , as well as pulmonologists having a special interest in diagnostic imaging and membership in the Brazilian Thoracic Association.
